Edo, Nigeria

Edo state is located in the southern region of Nigeria and is known for its rich cultural heritage and history. The state is home to the ancient Benin Kingdom, which is famous for its bronze sculptures and other artworks. Edo state is also known for its vibrant festivals, traditional music, and dance. The capital city, Benin City, is a major center for commerce and industry in the region. The people of Edo state are known for their hospitality and warmth, making it a great place to visit and experience Nigerian culture.
